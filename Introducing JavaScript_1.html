You really are getting the latest and best. Our goal - to give you a perfectly polished pool or spa ready for your leisure and pleasure.

Here are some pool perfection check points:

  1. PH:
    • Concrete pools - check that the PH is within the range of 7.2 to 7.6
    • Fibreglass pools - less tolerant of PH variation and alkalinity - check that the PH reading falls between 7.0 and 7.2
    • Check for possible interference - heavy downpours of rain, water replacement, heavy use of pool, introduction of chemicals that could upset PH
  2. Calcium Hardness:
    • Check that the calcium level falls between 250-350 ppm
    • Check for corrosion of metal parts or staining of pool
    • Check for calcium build-up on filters and pipes, and cloudiness of water
